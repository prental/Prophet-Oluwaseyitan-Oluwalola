Prophet Oluwaseyitan Oluwalola is a vessel chosen by God, anointed for such a time as this. With over 15 years of Spirit-led ministry, he has been used mightily by the Lord to transform lives, restore hope, and awaken destinies through prophetic insight, deep biblical revelation, and unwavering compassion. Called to raise a generation rooted in truth and fire, Prophet Oluwalola walks in obedience to the Holy Spirit, equipping the body of Christ and igniting revival in every place God sends him. His ministry is marked by powerful prayer, authentic worship, and a heart that burns for souls. Under his spiritual covering, we are building a sanctuary where God dwells — a place of prayer, purpose, and presence — where believers are empowered to grow in their walk with God and fulfill their divine calling.
